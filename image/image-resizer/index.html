Image Resizer

Resize images locally in your browser. Change dimensions, keep aspect ratio, preview the result, and download.

Privacy: processing happens locally in your browser. Your images are not uploaded.

Overview

Resize images safely in your browser. This tool redraws your image on a local canvas at the size you choose, then lets you preview and download the result. Use it for faster uploads, sharing, or fitting an exact width/height. Processing stays on your device.

How to use

  1. Drop an image file into the input area (or click to choose a file).
  2. Set the target width/height and choose whether to keep aspect ratio.
  3. Preview the resized image, then download it.

Privacy

This page is designed for fast, local processing in your browser. We don’t ask you to create an account, and we avoid uploading inputs to our server whenever possible.

FAQ

Does this tool upload my images?
No. Resizing runs locally in your browser. Files are not uploaded to our server.

Why is the downloaded file size different?
Different formats (PNG/JPG/WebP) and quality settings affect file size. Try WebP or lower quality for smaller files.

Can I resize without changing aspect ratio?
Turn off “Keep aspect ratio” to set width and height independently.
