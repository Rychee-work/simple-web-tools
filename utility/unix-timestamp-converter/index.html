Unix Timestamp Converter

Convert Unix timestamps (seconds/milliseconds) to date-time and back.

Tip: Explanations come first. The tool is below. Ads are placed last.

Overview

Convert between Unix timestamps and human-readable date/time instantly. Unix time is widely used in logs, APIs, and databases, but it can be hard to interpret at a glance. This converter supports both seconds and milliseconds, so it works with common formats from JavaScript and backend systems.
Paste a timestamp to see the corresponding date/time, or enter a date/time to get the Unix value. Everything runs locally in your browser for speed and privacy.

How to use

  1. Read the notes above so you know what the tool will do.
  2. Enter your input and click the main action button.
  3. Copy or download the result when you’re done.

Privacy
