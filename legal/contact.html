Contact

Need help, found a bug, or have a suggestion? Simple Web Tools is maintained by an individual developer. The fastest way to reach us is email. We read every message and use your feedback to improve tool accuracy, browser compatibility, and usability.

Why contact us

Contacting us is helpful when a tool behaves differently across browsers, when a file fails to process, when the output looks incorrect, or when you have a clear idea for improving the interface or documentation. If you include reproducible steps and the tool URL, we can usually identify whether it’s a browser limitation, a file constraint, or a bug in the tool logic and respond with a fix or a practical workaround.

Privacy & security of communications

We use your message only to respond to your inquiry and to improve the relevant tool or documentation. We do not sell your email, and we do not use it for unrelated marketing. If you attach screenshots or files, please avoid including sensitive personal information. You can also review our Privacy Policy for details about advertising and site analytics.

Before you send

Most tools run entirely in your browser (we do not upload your files to a server). If something looks wrong, it may be caused by browser restrictions, extensions, or large file sizes. Including the details below helps us reproduce the issue quickly.

Include in your message

  • The tool name and page URL
  • Steps to reproduce (what you clicked/typed)
  • What you expected vs what happened (include any error text)
  • Your browser and device (e.g., Chrome on Windows / Safari on iPhone)
  • Optional: screenshot (helps a lot)

Business inquiries

If you’re contacting us about partnerships, sponsorships, or licensing, please include your company name, country/region, and a short description of the request. We’ll reply from the same email thread.

Response time

We typically respond within a few business days. During weekends or high-volume periods, responses may take longer.
