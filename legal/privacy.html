Privacy Policy

Simple Web Tools (“we”, “us”) provides small online utilities. Many tools are designed to run locally in your browser, meaning your files are processed on your device and are not uploaded to our server.

1. What we collect

We may collect basic, non-sensitive technical data for security and performance purposes, such as: browser type, device type, approximate location (country/region), and pages visited. This data is typically collected via standard web logs and analytics.

2. Files you use with tools

When a tool runs locally, your files stay on your device. We do not intentionally upload your files to our servers. However, please note:

  • Some tools may use third-party libraries or services in the future. If a tool requires upload, the tool page will clearly indicate it before you proceed.
  • Your browser may store temporary files (cache) as part of normal operation.

3. Cookies and advertising

This site may display ads (for example via Google AdSense). Advertising providers may use cookies or similar technologies to deliver and measure ads, and (depending on your region/consent) personalize advertising.

You can control cookies via your browser settings and, where applicable, consent dialogs.

4. Third-party links

Our site may link to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review their policies.

5. Data retention

Log/analytics data is retained only as long as necessary for security, debugging, and basic reporting. We do not sell personal data.

6. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us via the Contact page.
