Overview

Explanation: X% of Y = (Y × X) ÷ 100. This tool updates instantly and runs fully in your browser.

FAQ

Quick answers to common questions.

What does “X% of Y” mean?
It means (Y × X) ÷ 100. This tool shows the value and the totals after increasing or decreasing Y by that value.
Does this tool upload my numbers?
No. Calculations run locally in your browser.
