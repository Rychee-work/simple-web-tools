Overview

Enter two integers to compute their GCD (greatest common divisor) and LCM (least common multiple).

Explanation: GCD uses the Euclidean algorithm. LCM = |a × b| ÷ GCD (when both are non-zero). Runs locally.

FAQ

Quick answers to common questions.

How is GCD computed?
Using the Euclidean algorithm: repeatedly replace (a, b) with (b, a mod b) until b is 0.
How is LCM computed?
LCM = |a × b| ÷ GCD (for non-zero integers).
